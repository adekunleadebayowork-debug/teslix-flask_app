About Teslix

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Teslix began as a small team of engineers passionate about sustainable energy solutions. Today, we've grown into a global leader in clean technology innovation.

Our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through cutting-edge technology and thoughtful design.
